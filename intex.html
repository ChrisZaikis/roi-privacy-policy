Privacy Policy

Controller: Ocysoft (“we”, “our”, “us”) • App: Roi - Period Tracker

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Roi - Period Tracker processes information when you use the app or associated services (collectively, the “Service”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

2. Data We Process

Roi is designed as a local-first app. The following information may be processed on your device:

  • Cycle data: period days, cycle length, period length, fertile window estimates, and related dates.
  • Symptoms & mood: items you choose to log (e.g., cramps, headache, mood, etc.).
  • Notes: free-text entries you voluntarily add.
  • App preferences: settings such as reminders, themes, and UI preferences.
  • Device permissions state: whether you have enabled notifications (for reminder delivery).

We do not require you to provide your name, email address, phone number, or precise location to use the core tracking features.

3. Data Storage & Security (Local-Only)

Your health information is stored locally on your device (for example, using device storage mechanisms such as local storage). We do not transmit or store your logged cycle, symptoms, mood, or notes on our servers unless you explicitly enable a future cloud feature.

  • You are responsible for device-level security (screen lock, OS updates, and physical access protection).
  • If you enable app lock (PIN/biometrics), you reduce the risk of unauthorized access.
  • No method of storage is 100% secure; however, we design Roi to minimize data exposure by keeping data on-device.

4. Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal health data with third parties for advertising or profiling.

5. Legal Bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we process information based on:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) to provide the Service features you request.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) where you enable optional features like notifications.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) for essential app functionality and security, limited to what is necessary.

Health data may be considered sensitive. Roi is designed to keep such data on your device and not transmit it to us.

6. Permissions

Roi may request the following permissions depending on your configuration:

  • Notifications: to send reminders and prediction alerts you configure.
  • Storage / Files: only if you use export/import backup features (to save or restore your data file).

You can enable or disable permissions at any time in your device settings. Core logging remains available without non-essential permissions.

7. Your Rights & Controls

You control your data directly in the app:

  • Access: view logged entries inside Roi.
  • Export: export your data (if the feature is available in your version).
  • Delete: delete your data by using in-app delete controls, clearing app storage, or uninstalling.
  • Portability: move data using backup/restore features (if available).

8. Children’s Privacy

Roi is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

9. Third-Party Services

Roi is designed to operate without third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. If you later add any third-party services (analytics, crash reporting, ads, cloud sync), this policy will be updated and you will be informed through the app or this page.
